Odia-\(\TeX\) may be viewed as a fork of the Oriya-\(\TeX\) project. Most of the hard work in designing the fonts has been done by Jeroen Hellingman in the Oriya-\(\TeX\) project. Anshuman Pandey has added some conjunctive consonants, written a C-based preprocessor (Copyright © 1996-2003, LPPL-GPL License). Here is the history of contributions to Oriya-\(\TeX\).

The main goal is to maintain and systematize the existing work, write elaborate user-friendly tutorials, write a user-friendly preprocessor, add missing conjunctive consonants. The github repository for Odia-\(\TeX\) may be viewed at https://github.com/nsoum/odia-tex. If you would like to contribute to this project and take it further along with me, feel free to email me at odia.bhashakosha [@] gmail [dot] com to discuss.
