About me

I am a postdoctoral researcher in bioinformatics at ITMAT, Smilow Center for Translational Research, Perelman School of Medicine, University of Pennsylvania mentored by Gregory Grant. I completed my Ph.D. in mathematics in August 2016 under the supervision of Richard V. Kadison in the area of operator algebras. Prior to that, I obtained my bachelor's degree in mathematics from the Indian Statistical Institute, Bangalore Centre.
