Mathematics Research

I am broadly interested in the theory of operator algebras and operator inequalities. I initially studied harmonic analysis, partial differential equations in graduate school passing my oral exams in those areas before moving to operator algebras for my dissertation. I have been interested in the theory of C*-convexity and its connections with representation theory and operator inequalities. More recently I have been dipping my toes into the theory of unbounded operators affiliated with a finite von Neumann algebra (Murray-von Neumann algebras).
