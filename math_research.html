Mathematics Research

I am broadly interested in the theory of operator algebras and operator inequalities. My research interests revolve around the phenomenon of convexity and in the mysterious ways it appears in operator algebras (and mathematics in the large), particularly in the context of the Schur-Horn theorem and its generalizations. Naturally, this goes hand in hand with the study of inequalities. Recently I have also been ruminating over some problems in classical convex geometry. In recent work, I have also explored structural aspects of Murray-von Neumann algebras, which are *-algebras of closed densely-defined operators affiliated with some finite von Neumann algebra.
