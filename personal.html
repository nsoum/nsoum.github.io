Odia-\(\TeX\)

Odia (ଓଡ଼ିଆ; also transliterated as Odiya, Oriya) is one of the six classical languages of India; the others being Kannada, Malayalam, Sanskrit, Tamil, Telugu. It is predominantly spoken in the state of Odisha (ଓଡ଼ିଶା).
The Odia-\(\TeX\) project is aimed at making \(\TeX\)-based professional typesetting in Odia, easier. For further details, please look here.The github repository for Odia-\(\TeX\) may be viewed at https://github.com/nsoum/odia-tex.
